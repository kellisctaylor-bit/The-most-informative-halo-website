The Halo Series

Why I Love Halo

Halo is a science fiction video game series developed originally by Bungie and now continued by 343 Industries. It follows humanity's fight against an alien collective known as the Covenant, and later against an ancient parasitic threat called the Flood. The series is known for its memorable soundtrack, deep lore, and the iconic protagonist, the Master Chief.

Main Games in the Series

Here is a breakdown of the main games and what you can do or find in each one:

  1. Halo: Combat Evolved (2001)
    • Introduced the Master Chief and Cortana
    • First appearance of the iconic Warthog vehicle
    • Featured the famous Halo ring structure
  2. Halo 2 (2004)
    • Introduced dual-wielding weapons
    • Added the Arbiter as a playable character
    • Popularized online console multiplayer
  3. Halo 3 (2007)
    • Concluded the original trilogy's story
    • Added the Forge map editor
    • Introduced equipment items like bubble shields
  4. Halo Infinite (2021)
    • Open-world style exploration
    • Free-to-play multiplayer mode
    • New grappleshot tool for movement
