Spartan Defense

Move with W A S D or the arrow keys. Aim with your mouse and click (or hold) to fire. Survive as many waves of Covenant grunts and elites as you can. Each wave gets faster and tougher. You have 5 lives — lose them all and it's game over.

Health: 5

Score: 0

Wave: 1

WASD / Arrow Keys to move  |  Mouse to aim  |  Click or hold Left Mouse to shoot
